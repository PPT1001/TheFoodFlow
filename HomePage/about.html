About Us

FoodFlow is a web application that enables the distribution of extra food for events and restaurants to those who need it. In order to create a cutting-edge food recovery and redistribution network that effectively distributes fresh and extremely perishable food, we act as an intermediary between senior care facilities, orphanages, and community involvement. We made the decision to feed people because Colombo is home to the second-highest number of orphanages and homes in Sri Lanka.

Vision & Mission

We aspire to bring up change to reduce food waste in Colombo and the surrounding area with the assistance of food establishments and event catering companies, our goal is to adequately feed those in need.
