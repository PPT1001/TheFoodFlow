About Us

FoodFlow is a web application that enables the distribution of extra food for events and restaurants to those who need it. In order to create a cutting-edge food recovery and redistribution network that effectively distributes fresh and extremely perishable food, we act as an intermediary between senior care facilities, orphanages, and community involvement. Vision & Mission

Our vision at FoodFlow aspire to bring up change to reduce food waste in Colombo and the surrounding area with the assistance of food establishments and event catering companies, our goal is to adequately feed those in need.
Our mission is to bridge the gap between surplus food sources and those who are hungry or in need, transforming excess into opportunity. We are committed to Minimizing Food Waste,Raising Awareness,Fostering Innovation,and Nourishing Communities.
